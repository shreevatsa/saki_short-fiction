# Saki Short Stories - Annotated List

## Legend

### Quality & Tone
- **Stars**: 1-5 quality rating (plot & characters)
- **Tone**: F=Funny, S=Serious, M=Mixed

### Original Themes
- **Children**: Child triumphs over adult
- **Animals**: Wild animals / nature threat
- **Lying**: Child makes up stories / lies
- **Prank**: Practical joke
- **Super**: Supernatural elements
- **Twist**: Twist/ironic ending

### Plot Types (NEW)
- **Con**: Con artist / swindle scheme
- **Blackmail**: Blackmail as plot element
- **MisID**: Mistaken identity / impersonation
- **Revenge**: Revenge / comeuppance
- **Death**: Unexpected death occurs

### Social Satire (NEW)
- **Aunts**: Terrible aunts
- **Climbers**: Social climbers mocked
- **Politics**: Politicians / political satire
- **Clergy**: Clergy / religion mocked
- **Suff**: Suffragettes mentioned/mocked
- **Art**: Art world pretensions

### Structural (NEW)
- **Frame**: Frame narrative (story told by a character)
- **Sketch**: Sketch/monologue (vs proper plot)
- **Fable**: Fable/allegory

### Tone Details (NEW)
- **Dark**: Dark/cruel ending
- **Dies**: Someone dies in the story
- **Unsettle**: Genuinely unsettling

### Series Characters (NEW)
- **Clovis**: Clovis Sangrail appears
- **Reginald**: Reginald story
